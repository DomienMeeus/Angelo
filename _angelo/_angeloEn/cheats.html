BEWARE of CHEATS!

NEVER accept proposals to buy our products sent to you by email!

Recently, some impostors are sending emails to try and sell you products like ours. If you accept their proposals, you will definitely lose your money! These crooks will disappear (with your money) and you will receive NOTHING!

How do they do this?

These frauds present themselves as Angelo Brazilian Hair to try and sell you various products. You receive a professional looking email containing a product presentation and interesting rates. At the bottom of the message you can indeed see our logo, shop address, telephone and fax number as well as the website address. You will be required to forward your order to a Gmail or Hotmail address. However, this is an email we have nothing to do with!

When you order something, relying on our good renown, you will be asked to pay via Western Union. Once the payment is done, you will hear nothing anymore. Obviously, you won’t receive any of the products you ordered.

This malicious activity surfaced after a number of worried clients were calling us to check when they would receive their order. Meanwhile, we have reported the swindling to the police, but please stay cautious until the crooks are caught!

WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW

1. For a start, Angelo Brazilian Hair will NEVER attempt to contact you without being asked to do so, and even less so via email. If you do get an email from us, it must be the impostors. Never reply to them and certainly do not order anything.

2. All of our online communication happens through an email address with our own domain (brazilianhair.be) and never via some Gmail or Hotmail account (or any other), which can be made by anyone. Hence, an email that looks like this: name@brazilanhair.be is correct. Email addresses such as name@gmail.com or name@hotmail.com are ALWAYS FAKE!

3. If you wish to place an order, please do so exclusively via the official contact form on the website or send it by email to info@brazilianhair.be.

If you happen to receive a suspicious email, do not hesitate to inform us about it.
